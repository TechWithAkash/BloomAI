Tips for Identifying Flowers

Take Clear Photos

Make sure your flower is well-lit and the image is not blurry.

Focus on the Flower

Center the flower in your image and try to include the whole bloom.

Natural Light

Take photos in natural daylight for the best results.

Include Features

If possible, include leaves, stems, and other distinctive features.
